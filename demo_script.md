# ESGnie: 1-Minute Demo Video Script

**Theme:** Empowering SMEs for ESG Reporting
**Tone:** Professional, Efficient, and Accessible
**Duration:** 60 Seconds

---

## [0:00 - 0:10] Introduction
**Visual:** Close-up of a stressed SME owner looking at a pile of utility bills and a complex ESG spreadsheet.
**Audio (Voiceover):** "ESG reporting is no longer just for big corporations. But for SMEs, gathering data can feel like a mountain of paperwork."
**Text Overlay:** ESG Reporting: The SME Challenge.

## [0:10 - 0:20] The Solution
**Visual:** Transition to the ESGnie interface. Clean, modern, and inviting.
**Audio (Voiceover):** "Meet ESGnie—your AI-powered pre-audit companion designed to simplify sustainability reporting."
**Text Overlay:** ESGnie: Your Pre-Audit Partner.

## [0:20 - 0:35] Automated Extraction (The "Magic")
**Visual:** Screen recording of a TNB (Electricity) bill being dragged into the upload zone. The AI agent starts scanning, and the kWh values automatically populate the form.
**Audio (Voiceover):** "Stop manual entry. Just upload your utility bills. Our AI instantly extracts electricity, fuel, and water data with precision—even identifying your company name automatically."
**Text Overlay:** Automated OCR Data Extraction.

## [0:35 - 0:50] AI Analysis & Reporting
**Visual:** The user clicks 'Launch ESGnie Analysis'. A professional report generates with charts and AI-driven recommendations.
**Audio (Voiceover):** "ESGnie doesn't just collect data; it analyzes it. Get instant insights into your carbon footprint and social impact, helping you identify gaps before the official audit."
**Text Overlay:** Pre-Audit Readiness.

## [0:50 - 1:00] Closing & CTA
**Visual:** The SME owner now looking confident, holding a tablet with a completed ESG report. Logo appears.
**Audio (Voiceover):** "Prepare for the future of business. ESGnie: Making ESG reporting simple, accurate, and audit-ready for every SME."
**Text Overlay:** Start Your ESG Journey Today. [App URL]

---

### Production Tips:
*   **Background Music:** Upbeat, tech-focused, and inspiring.
*   **Pacing:** Keep the transitions snappy to fit the 60-second limit.
*   **Focus:** Highlight the 'Auto-fill' feature as it's the biggest time-saver for SMEs.
